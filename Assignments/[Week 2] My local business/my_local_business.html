FADIL VOKRRI Stadium

The Fadil Vokrri Stadium (Albanian: Stadiumi Fadil Vokrri), formerly known as Prishtina City Stadium (Albanian: Stadiumi i qytetit të Prishtinës), is a versatile stadium located in Prishtina, Kosovo. It primarily serves as the home ground for FC Prishtina and the Kosovo national football team. The stadium boasts a seating capacity of 13,980 spectators.
